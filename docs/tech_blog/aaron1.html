Welcome!¶

My name is Aaron Smusz and I have been working with Adobe Sign since 2012.

I’m a “Customer Enablement Engineer”, which is a buzz-word for someone who helps people figure out how to use this technology in their business workflows, and works with our engineering and product management teams to bring the customer’s perspective into clearer focus while we make decisions about how to improve all things e-Signature.

During most of my time working with Adobe Sign, I have been helping customers install, configure and adopt many of the “Out of Box” integrations available for Adobe Sign. Some are developed by Adobe, some by our partners. I have also helped many who are interested in building an integration for Adobe Sign, develop and implement those new integrations. This may be a partner who needs to provide “eSignature” functionality to their customers as part of their platform “workflows” or, in other cases, a customer who needs this same type of functionality included in their own “homegrown” app, or some other “off-the-shelf” app/process they use which does not already have a pre-built integration.

Have fun!
